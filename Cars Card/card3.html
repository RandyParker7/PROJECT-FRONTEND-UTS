Ferrari 296 GT3

Price: Rp 9.383.485.500,00

about this car:

Engine: Ferrari F163CE V6 GDI Turbo

Power: 600 hp (591.792 bhp) (441.299 kw)

Fuel Capacity: 104.10 liters (27.5 gallons)

Weight: 1350 kg (2976 lbs)

Top Speed: 177 mph (285 kmh)

0-60 mph (0-100 kmh) in 3.0 seconds

Type: Sports car (S)

Year: 2023

Configuration: Mid-Engine RWD

Bio: Newest GT3 racer to come to iRacing as of September 2023, the Ferrari 296 GT3 is the newest of Ferrari's GT3 racing machines ready to tear up the track on raceday. The car comes with many features, components and more, one of them being its engine, a Ferrari F163CE V6 GDI Turbo packing 600 horsepower. Other features include a Transversal sequential gearbox with an 6 speed e-clutch actuator transmission and a double wishbone, tubular steel suspension arm with aluminum uprights and even adjustable dampers. This gives the car ample suspension, durability, toughness, and a smooth transition for shifting through the gears. The last feature on this car to point out is that it also has brake calipers with the fronts having 6 pistons and the rear having 4 pistons helping the car to slow down or stop whenever its needed. No matter what though, this Ferrari 296 GT3 is ready to race.
